An oceanfront boutique hotel experience

The Chateau Mar Beach Resort introduces a refreshing blend of modern simplicity and understated elegance to "the world's most famous beach". Following an extensive renovation in 2015, we present to you a refreshing boutique oceanfront location offering luxury accommodations and a relaxing pool area, all just steps from the beach. The Chateau Mar Beach Resort provides the perfect mix of style and comfort, along with a personalized quality of service aimed at enhancing your travel experience. We work hard everyday to create a truly unique destination for our clients. Let us show you what makes the Chateau Mar Beach Resort different than the rest. Come Dream With Us.

Our Accommodations

Experience contemporary design in an intimate boutique hotel setting at The Chateau Mar Beach Resort. The recently completed multi-million dollar renovation of the hotel exterior and interior sets the stage for a unique level of simple elegance and personalized service. Our Oceanfront and Ocean View rooms offer breathtaking views of Ormond Beach and the Atlantic Ocean, and our newly updated pool deck provides our guests with the ideal location to sit back, relax, and enjoy.
